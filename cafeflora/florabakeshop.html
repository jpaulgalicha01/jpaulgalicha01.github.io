Freshly baked pastries with local, seasonal ingredients.


Fresh, local, vibrant vegetarian fare to fuel your journey ahead.

The newest addition to our family, the Flora Bakehouse is the perfect connection between Cafe Flora and Floret. This sun-filled cafe brings the Beacon Hill neighborhood freshly baked sweet and savory pastries, freshly baked bread, and inventive espresso drinks each and every day. From twice baked almond croissants, kouign amann and farmers market inspired danishes and savory croissants to our selection of gluten-free and vegan delights including our famous vegan cinnamon rolls, there is something for everyone! Freshly baked loaves of bread are now available to purchase seven days a week! Our rooftop deck is now open for your enjoyment, complete with Adirondack chairs, shaded plus full-sun areas, and a perfect view of Mt. Rainier. Also enjoy carefully curated natural wines, local beers, and other cold beverages from our Bakehouse Bottle Shop.

Celebrate your next occasion with us in our private event space!

Our event space is located upstairs at The Flora Bakehouse and includes our picturesque roof deck with a gorgeous view of Mt. Rainier in the distance as well as our indoor event space. The deck is partially covered and offers a wide variety of seating options as well as our intimate indoor seating area.


Our catering menu offers everything from english teas to roman-style pizza parties. We proudly source fresh, local, and seasonally-inspired ingredients and look forward to working with you to create a beautiful event.


For questions, please reach out to events@florabakehouse.com
