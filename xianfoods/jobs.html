JOIN THE BEST

Our work family is composed of the most capable individuals who are carefully selected based on their qualifications, comprehensively trained to our high standards, and constantly involved in shaping the growth of our world-class company.



Xi'an Famous Foods is a successful business that believes in the success of its employees as individuals. As such, we are an industry leader when it comes to compensation and benefits. To illustrate with our most entry-level position: our Store Associates' wages are among the highest in the industry at $21/hour starting rate (up to $24/hour base rate based on training, before any seniority raises), with skill-based raises as well as automatic raises based on seniority, and comprehensive health/dental/vision insurance and 401K plan for all eligible employees, just to name some benefits. Associates are encouraged to learn valuable skills and grow with on-the-job work experience. Associates have the opportunity to be promoted from within and to be offered opportunities to actually run their own business as managers, and be rewarded as such.
